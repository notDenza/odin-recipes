Fluffy Pancakes


Prep10 min
Rest10 min
Cook15 min
Total35 min
Makes10 pancakes

Description :

These pancakes are light, fluffy, and really easy to make with simple ingredients that you probably already have in your kitchen.

They are perfect for a lazy weekend breakfast, and you can top them with anything you like — maple syrup, fresh berries, or a dusting of powdered sugar.

Ingredients :

  • 1 ½ cups (190 g) all-purpose flour
  • 1 ¼ cups (300 ml) milk
  • 1 large egg
  • 2 tbsp sugar
  • 3 ½ tsp baking powder
  • 3 tbsp butter, melted (plus more for the pan)
  • ¼ tsp salt
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract

Steps :

  1. In a large bowl, whisk together the flour, sugar, baking powder, and salt until evenly combined.
  2. In a separate bowl, whisk the milk, egg, melted butter, and vanilla until smooth.
  3. Pour the wet mixture into the dry ingredients and stir gently until just combined. A few small lumps are fine — do not overmix or the pancakes will turn out tough.
  4. Let the batter rest for 10 minutes so the baking powder activates and the gluten relaxes.
  5. Heat a non-stick pan or griddle over medium heat and lightly grease it with butter. The pan is ready when a drop of water sizzles.
  6. Pour about ¼ cup of batter per pancake onto the pan, leaving space between each.
  7. Cook for 2–3 minutes, until bubbles form across the surface and the edges look set.
  8. Flip the pancake with a spatula in one confident motion.
  9. Cook the other side for 1–2 minutes, until golden brown.
  10. Transfer to a warm plate, repeat with the remaining batter, and serve with your favorite toppings.
