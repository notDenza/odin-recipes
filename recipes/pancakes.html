Best Fluffy Pancakes Recipe


Description :

These pancakes are light, fluffy, and really easy to make with simple ingredients that you probably already have in your kitchen.

They are perfect for a lazy weekend breakfast, and you can top them with anything you like:

ingredients :

  • Flour
  • Milk
  • Egg
  • Sugar
  • Baking powder
  • Butter
  • Salt
  • Vanilla

Steps:

  1. Mix the dry ingredients.
  2. Whisk the milk, egg, and butter.
  3. Combine the wet and dry mixtures.
  4. Let the batter rest.
  5. Heat a pan with a little butter.
  6. Pour the batter into the pan.
  7. Wait for the bubbles to form.
  8. Flip the pancake.
  9. Cook the other side.
  10. Serve with toppings.
