Traditional Algerian Rechta


Prep45 min
Steam45 min
Cook50 min
Total~2 hr 20 min
Serves4–6

Description :

Rechta is a traditional Algerian dish made of thin handmade noodles served with a light cinnamon-scented chicken sauce and tender vegetables.

It is often made for special occasions and family gatherings, and these are the things you will need:

Ingredients :

  • 500 g (4 cups) flour, plus oil for coating
  • 250 ml (1 cup) water and 1 tsp salt for the dough
  • 1 whole chicken, cut into pieces
  • 1 large onion, grated
  • 200 g (1 cup) chickpeas, soaked overnight
  • 2 turnips, peeled and quartered
  • 1 cinnamon stick + 1 tsp ground cinnamon, salt and pepper

Steps :

  1. Make the noodle dough: mix the flour, salt, and water, then knead for 10 minutes until smooth. Cover and rest 20 minutes.
  2. Roll the dough out very thin, brushing lightly with oil to keep it from sticking.
  3. Fold and cut the dough into very thin strips to form the rechta noodles.
  4. Steam the noodles in a couscoussier (steamer) for 45 minutes, fluffing them with oil between rounds, until soft.
  5. In a large pot, cook the chicken pieces with the grated onion and a splash of oil over medium heat for 10 minutes, until lightly colored.
  6. Add the cinnamon stick, ground cinnamon, salt, pepper, and enough water to cover. Bring to a simmer.
  7. Add the soaked chickpeas and the turnips to the pot.
  8. Simmer gently for 40 minutes, until the chicken is tender and the sauce has thickened slightly.
  9. Arrange the steamed noodles on a platter and pour the hot sauce, chicken, and vegetables over the top.
  10. Serve warm, dusted with a little extra cinnamon.
