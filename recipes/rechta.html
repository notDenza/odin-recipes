Traditional Algerian Rechta


Description :

Rechta is a traditional Algerian dish made of thin handmade noodles served with a light cinnamon-scented chicken sauce and tender vegetables.

It is often made for special occasions and family gatherings, and these are the things you will need:

Ingredients :

  • Flour
  • Water
  • Salt
  • Chicken
  • Onion
  • Chickpeas
  • Turnip
  • Cinnamon

Steps :

  1. Make the dough for the noodles.
  2. Roll the dough very thin.
  3. Cut the dough into thin strips.
  4. Steam the noodles until soft.
  5. Cook the chicken with the onion.
  6. Add the spices and water.
  7. Add the chickpeas and turnip.
  8. Simmer the sauce.
  9. Pour the sauce over the noodles.
  10. Serve warm.
