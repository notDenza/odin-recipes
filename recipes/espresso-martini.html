Smooth Espresso Martini


Prep3 min
Brew2 min
Shake30 sec
Total~6 min
Makes1 cocktail

Description :

This espresso martini is rich, smooth, and lightly sweet, with a bold coffee flavor and a beautiful foamy top.

It is the perfect drink to enjoy after dinner, and these are the things you will need:

Ingredients :

  • 2 oz (60 ml) vodka
  • 1 oz (30 ml) coffee liqueur (e.g. Kahlúa)
  • 1 oz (30 ml) fresh espresso
  • ½ oz (15 ml) sugar syrup
  • 1 cup ice cubes
  • 3 coffee beans, to garnish

Steps :

  1. Brew a single shot (1 oz) of fresh espresso.
  2. Let the espresso cool for 1–2 minutes so it doesn't melt the ice too fast.
  3. Fill a cocktail shaker with ice cubes.
  4. Pour in the vodka and coffee liqueur.
  5. Add the cooled espresso and the sugar syrup.
  6. Seal the shaker and shake hard for 20–30 seconds — this is what builds the signature foam.
  7. Double-strain into a chilled martini glass.
  8. Let the foam settle for a few seconds until it forms an even layer.
  9. Garnish with three coffee beans floated on top.
  10. Serve right away, while still cold and foamy.
