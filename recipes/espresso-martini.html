Smooth Espresso Martini


Description :

This espresso martini is rich, smooth, and lightly sweet, with a bold coffee flavor and a beautiful foamy top.

It is the perfect drink to enjoy after dinner, and these are the things you will need:

Ingredients :

  • Vodka
  • Coffee liqueur
  • Espresso
  • Sugar syrup
  • Ice
  • Coffee beans

Steps :

  1. Brew a shot of fresh espresso.
  2. Let the espresso cool a little.
  3. Fill a shaker with ice.
  4. Pour in the vodka and coffee liqueur.
  5. Add the espresso and sugar syrup.
  6. Shake everything hard.
  7. Strain into a chilled glass.
  8. Wait for the foam to form.
  9. Garnish with coffee beans.
  10. Serve right away.
