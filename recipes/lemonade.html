Fresh Classic Lemonade


Description :

This homemade lemonade is sweet, sour, and very refreshing, which makes it the perfect drink for a hot summer day.

It only takes a few minutes to make, and these are the things you will need:

Ingredients :

  • Lemons
  • Water
  • Sugar
  • Ice
  • Mint

Steps :

  1. Squeeze the lemons.
  2. Make a simple sugar syrup.
  3. Mix the lemon juice and syrup.
  4. Add the cold water.
  5. Stir everything together.
  6. Taste and adjust the sugar.
  7. Add the ice cubes.
  8. Garnish with mint.
  9. Serve cold.
