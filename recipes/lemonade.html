Fresh Classic Lemonade


Prep10 min
Cook5 min
Chill30 min
Total45 min
Serves6 glasses

Description :

This homemade lemonade is sweet, sour, and very refreshing, which makes it the perfect drink for a hot summer day.

It only takes a few minutes to make, and these are the things you will need:

Ingredients :

  • 1 cup (240 ml) fresh lemon juice (about 5–6 lemons)
  • 5 cups (1.2 L) cold water
  • 1 cup (200 g) sugar
  • 1 cup ice cubes
  • 1 handful fresh mint leaves

Steps :

  1. Roll the lemons firmly on the counter, cut them in half, and squeeze out 1 cup of juice. Strain out the seeds.
  2. Make a simple syrup: combine the sugar with 1 cup of the water in a small saucepan and heat over medium, stirring, for 3–5 minutes until fully dissolved. Let it cool slightly.
  3. In a large pitcher, mix the lemon juice and the warm sugar syrup together.
  4. Add the remaining 4 cups of cold water.
  5. Stir everything together until well combined.
  6. Taste and adjust — add more sugar if too sour, or more lemon if too sweet.
  7. Chill in the fridge for 30 minutes, then add the ice cubes.
  8. Garnish with fresh mint leaves and a few lemon slices.
  9. Serve cold and enjoy.
