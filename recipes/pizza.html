Classic Pizza Napoletana


Description :

This Neapolitan pizza is simple, soft, and full of flavor, with a thin charred crust and just a few fresh toppings that let the dough shine.

It comes straight from Naples, and these are the ingredients you will need:

Ingredients :

  • Flour
  • Water
  • Yeast
  • Salt
  • Tomatoes
  • Mozzarella
  • Basil
  • Olive oil

Steps :

  1. Mix the flour, water, yeast, and salt.
  2. Knead the dough well.
  3. Let the dough rise.
  4. Divide the dough into balls.
  5. Stretch the dough by hand.
  6. Spread the tomato sauce.
  7. Add the mozzarella and basil.
  8. Drizzle with olive oil.
  9. Bake in a very hot oven.
  10. Serve hot.
