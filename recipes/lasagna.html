Authentic Lasagna


Prep30 min
Cook1 hr 15 min
Rest15 min
Total2 hr
Serves8

Description :

The Allrecipes community adores this lasagna recipe because it's incredibly customizable, so you can easily alter the ingredient list to suit your needs.

If you want to stay true to the original recipe, though, these are the ingredients you'll need to add to your grocery list:

Ingredients :

  • 1 lb (450 g) ground beef
  • 1 onion, chopped, and 3 garlic cloves, minced
  • 28 oz (800 g) crushed tomatoes + 6 oz (170 g) tomato paste
  • 1 tbsp sugar
  • 2 tsp dried basil, 1 tsp oregano, salt and pepper to taste
  • 12 lasagna noodles
  • 15 oz (425 g) ricotta, 3 cups (340 g) mozzarella, ½ cup parmesan
  • 1 egg

Steps :

  1. In a large pot over medium heat, cook the ground beef, onion, and garlic for 8–10 minutes, until the meat is browned. Drain off excess fat.
  2. Stir in the crushed tomatoes, tomato paste, sugar, basil, oregano, salt, and pepper.
  3. Cover and simmer the sauce on low for 30 minutes, stirring occasionally.
  4. Meanwhile, boil the lasagna noodles in salted water for 8–10 minutes until just tender, then drain.
  5. In a bowl, mix the ricotta, parmesan, and egg with a pinch of salt to make the cheese filling.
  6. Spread a thin layer of sauce over the bottom of a 9×13 inch baking dish.
  7. Layer over it: noodles, half the ricotta mixture, a third of the mozzarella, and a layer of sauce.
  8. Repeat the layers once more, finishing with a final layer of noodles.
  9. Top with the remaining sauce and mozzarella. Cover with foil.
  10. Bake at 375°F (190°C) for 25 minutes, then uncover and bake 20 more minutes until bubbly and golden. Let it rest 15 minutes before slicing.
