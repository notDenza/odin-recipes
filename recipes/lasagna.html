World's Best Lasagna Recipe


Description :

The Allrecipes community adores this lasagna recipe because it's incredibly customizable, so you can easily alter the ingredient list to suit your needs.

If you want to stay true to the original recipe, though, these are the ingredients you'll need to add to your grocery list:

Ingredients :

  • Meat
  • Onion and garlic
  • Tomato products
  • Sugar
  • Spices and seasonings
  • Lasagna noodles
  • Cheeses
  • Egg

Steps :

  1. Cook meat, onion, and garlic.
  2. Add tomatoes and seasonings.
  3. Simmer the sauce.
  4. Boil the lasagna noodles.
  5. Prepare the cheese mixture.
  6. Spread sauce in the dish.
  7. Layer noodles and cheeses.
  8. Repeat the layers.
  9. Top with sauce and cheese.
  10. Bake and let cool.
